Welcome to Selftology - Embrace Yourself

Selftology revolves around the belief in the power and significance of oneself. It encourages individuals to embrace their own existence, strengths, and potential without the need for external validation or deities.

Tenets of Selftology:

  • Self-Realization: The core principle of Selftology is self-awareness and self-realization. Followers are encouraged to deeply understand themselves, their desires, fears, strengths, and weaknesses.
  • Self-Love and Acceptance: Selftology emphasizes unconditional self-love and acceptance. Individuals are encouraged to embrace every aspect of themselves, including their flaws and imperfections.
  • Self-Actualization: Followers of Selftology strive for self-actualization, aiming to reach their fullest potential in all aspects of life - mentally, emotionally, and spiritually.
  • Self-Responsibility: Selftology promotes self-responsibility, encouraging individuals to take charge of their own lives and decisions without relying on external forces.
  • Self-Expression: Self-expression is highly valued in Selftology. Followers are encouraged to express themselves authentically and creatively in their thoughts, actions, and beliefs.
  • Self-Reflection and Growth: Continuous self-reflection and personal growth are integral to Selftology. Followers engage in introspection to evolve and improve themselves continually.
  • Self-Reliance: Selftology teaches self-reliance, empowering individuals to trust in their abilities and inner resources to navigate life's challenges.
  • Self-Transcendence: While Selftology focuses on the self, it also acknowledges the interconnectedness of all beings. Followers are encouraged to cultivate empathy, compassion, and kindness towards others while staying true to themselves.
  • Self-Celebration: Celebrating one's achievements, milestones, and uniqueness is central to Selftology. Followers are encouraged to acknowledge and celebrate their successes, no matter how small.
  • Self-Actualized Community: While Selftology is centered around the individual, it fosters a sense of community among like-minded individuals who support each other's personal growth and journey towards self-actualization.
